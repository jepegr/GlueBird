# How to Glue Foam to Metal

**Quick answer:** Foam-Safe Adhesive is the adhesive to reach for. Craft glue also works if you can clamp the joint while it dries; just avoid contact cement and CA, both attack foam.

## Why This Combination Needs the Right Adhesive

Metal offers zero forgiveness on prep — any oil, dust, or oxidation on the surface will undermine even the right foam-safe adhesive.

## Step-by-Step Tips

- Degrease the metal with alcohol before bonding — foam won't show the mistake the way wood grain does, but the bond will still fail.
- Use light, even clamping pressure across the whole foam surface, not just the edges.
- Let it cure fully before any flexing or handling — foam bonds are more fragile early on than they look.
