# How to Glue Wood to Glass

**Quick answer:** Silicone Sealant is the adhesive to reach for. Silicone flexes with wood's seasonal movement, so the glass won't crack when the wood expands or contracts.

## Why This Combination Needs the Right Adhesive

Wood expands and contracts with humidity far more than most people expect — a rigid glue line between wood and glass eventually transfers that movement straight into the glass.

## Step-by-Step Tips

- Choose 100% silicone over a blended caulk for the strongest, most flexible bond.
- Tool the bead smooth before it skins over — cleanup after curing is much harder.
- Give it the full cure time before handling; silicone stays tacky far longer than it looks.
