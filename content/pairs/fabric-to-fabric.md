# How to Glue Fabric to Fabric

**Quick answer:** Fabric / Leather Adhesive is the adhesive to reach for. Apply a thin, even line and press with a roller rather than fingers, or the bond telegraphs through as a stiff ridge.

## Why This Combination Needs the Right Adhesive

A seam glued too thick doesn't just look bad — it changes how the fabric drapes and moves, which is often more noticeable than the glue line itself.

## Step-by-Step Tips

- Test on a fabric offcut first — some dyes react visibly to certain adhesive formulas.
- Let the glue cure fully flat before folding or wearing the piece.
- For anything that will be washed, check the label for a washable/permanent-bond formula specifically.
