# How to Glue Flexible Plastic to Flexible Plastic

**Quick answer:** Contact Cement is the adhesive to reach for. Vinyl and other flexible plastics need a bond that flexes with them, or the seam cracks at the first fold.

## Why This Combination Needs the Right Adhesive

Flexible plastics like vinyl are engineered to bend repeatedly without fatigue — but a rigid adhesive seam becomes the one spot that can't flex, and that's exactly where it fails first.

## Step-by-Step Tips

- Clean with alcohol first — flexible plastics often carry a light surface coating from manufacturing.
- Apply contact cement to both surfaces and let it dry tacky before joining, same as with rubber.
- Avoid rigid CA or epoxy here even though they bond fast — the seam won't survive normal flexing.
