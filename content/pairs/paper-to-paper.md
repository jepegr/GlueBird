# How to Glue Paper to Paper

**Quick answer:** All-Purpose Craft Glue is the adhesive to reach for. A glue stick avoids the wrinkling that liquid craft glue causes on thin paper.

## Why This Combination Needs the Right Adhesive

Paper wrinkles when it absorbs moisture unevenly — which is exactly what a wet line of liquid glue does, even though the paper looks dry again once it's cured.

## Step-by-Step Tips

- Press flat under a book or in a press while it dries to keep the paper from curling.
- For heavier cardstock, a thin layer of PVA-based craft glue works fine and dries faster than you'd expect.
- Avoid saturating the edges — that's where visible wrinkling shows up first.
