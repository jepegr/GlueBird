# How to Glue Cork to Cork

**Quick answer:** All-Purpose Craft Glue is the adhesive to reach for. Spread thin; cork's texture already gives the glue plenty to key into.

## Why This Combination Needs the Right Adhesive

Cork's natural cell structure is full of tiny air pockets, which is part of why it's such an easy, forgiving surface for adhesives to grip onto.

## Step-by-Step Tips

- Weight the pieces evenly rather than clamping hard, since cork compresses easily.
- Let it cure fully before trimming or sanding the edges flush.
- For cork coasters or boards, a slightly thicker tacky glue holds position better while drying.
