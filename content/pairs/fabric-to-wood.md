# How to Glue Fabric to Wood

**Quick answer:** Fabric / Leather Adhesive is the adhesive to reach for. Fabric glue stays flexible where wood glue would crack the cloth at the fold line.

## Why This Combination Needs the Right Adhesive

Wood glue dries rigid, which works fine on two stiff surfaces — but against fabric, that rigidity becomes the exact spot where the cloth cracks or tears at the fold.

## Step-by-Step Tips

- Lightly sand a finished or sealed wood surface first so the adhesive has something to grip.
- Apply glue to the wood side and press the fabric on, rather than soaking the fabric directly.
- Weight the piece flat while curing to prevent puckering.
