# How to Glue Stone to Metal

**Quick answer:** 2-Part Epoxy is the adhesive to reach for. A slow-set epoxy gives you time to align heavy stone before it grabs.

## Why This Combination Needs the Right Adhesive

Stone's irregular, porous surface and metal's smooth, nonporous one need an adhesive that can handle both textures in the same joint — general-purpose glue rarely manages it.

## Step-by-Step Tips

- Degrease and lightly roughen the metal side before bonding.
- Brace the stone in position rather than relying on the adhesive to hold it while curing.
- For anything outdoor or load-bearing, confirm the epoxy is rated for structural, exterior use.
