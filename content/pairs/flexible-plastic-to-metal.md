# How to Glue Flexible Plastic to Metal

**Quick answer:** 2-Part Epoxy is the adhesive to reach for. Clean the plastic with alcohol first — mould-release residue is the most common reason this joint fails.

## Why This Combination Needs the Right Adhesive

Flexible plastics are often manufactured with a thin mold-release coating that's invisible to the eye but is enough to stop most adhesives from ever really touching the surface.

## Step-by-Step Tips

- Lightly roughen both surfaces after cleaning for extra grip.
- Use an epoxy rated for flexible substrates if the joint will see regular movement.
- Clamp lightly rather than tightly — flexible plastic deforms under heavy clamping pressure.
