# How to Glue Rigid Plastic to Rigid Plastic

**Quick answer:** Cyanoacrylate (Super Glue) is the adhesive to reach for. Check the plastic first — CA glue doesn't bond polypropylene or polyethylene at all, no matter the brand.

## Why This Combination Needs the Right Adhesive

Not all plastics are chemically alike — polypropylene and polyethylene have low surface energy that makes them almost universally glue-resistant without a specialty primer, regardless of brand or price.

## Step-by-Step Tips

- Look for a recycling code stamped on the plastic (PP is 5, PE is 2 or 4) if you're unsure what you're working with.
- For those resistant plastics, use a specialty plastic-bonding primer or activator system instead.
- Clean the surface with alcohol before bonding; mold-release residue is a common cause of failed joints.
