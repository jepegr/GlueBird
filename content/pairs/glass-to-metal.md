# How to Glue Glass to Metal

**Quick answer:** 2-Part Epoxy is the adhesive to reach for. Epoxy bridges the different expansion rates of glass and metal better than CA glue, which can pop loose once it's jarred.

## Why This Combination Needs the Right Adhesive

Glass and metal expand and contract at noticeably different rates as temperature changes. A rigid, brittle adhesive can't absorb that mismatch — it just transfers the stress straight into the glass until it cracks.

## Step-by-Step Tips

- Roughen the metal side with fine sandpaper first — epoxy needs texture to grip, glass doesn't.
- Clean both surfaces with alcohol to remove oils before bonding; glass shows every skipped step.
- Use a slower-curing epoxy for anything larger than a small repair — it gives you time to align before it grabs.
